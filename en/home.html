Welcome to SNSWiki!

Welcome to SNSWiki! This is a project intended to document, archive and spread the word about controversies, incidents and other tragic events directly caused by the government's incompetence.
Of course this site isn't exclusive to SNS-caused events but considering they were the only ones that got a chance to ruin Serbia in the past 12 years, this site was named accordingly.
If you are interested in contributing you can:
  • Edit and create new articles, a guide to help you with that is located here.
  • You can help with developing the site itself, if you are familiar with web development, here.
